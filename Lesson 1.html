Geometric Properties of Triangles

Properties of Triangles

Property

Have 3 sides and 3 angles

The combined length of any 2 sides of a triangle is ALWAYS greater than the length of the 3rd side.

The interior angles of a triangle always add up (sum) to 180°

The exterior angles of a triangle always add up (sum) to 360°

Triangle Angles

Understand the question I can see that ∠A is an interior angle and ∠a is the corresponding exterior angle. I know that when I add up all the interior angles of a triangle they equal to 180°. I know that adding the interior and corresponding exterior angle always equals to 180°

Explaining Triangle Angles

∠A + 50° + 45° = 180°
∠A + 95° = 180°
∠A = 85°
∠A + ∠a = 180°
85° + ∠a = 180°
∠a = 95°
Therefore, ∠A = 85° and ∠a = 95°
